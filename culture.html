Indian Culture

India is a diverse nation full of diverse culture in different states. Gujarat is famous for the famous 'Garba' dance, West Bengal is famous for The Durga Puja, Ganesh Chaturthy is celebrated with great jeal in Maharashtra. Not only festivals, but also India is really great in establishing friendship bond between people of all kinds who come to visit India. Diverse clothing, awesome food, traditional customs , rich humanity is all that makes India one of the most culturally prosperous nations of the world !
